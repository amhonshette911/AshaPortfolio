Design and development of innovative vertical axis wind turbine.

Wind turbines are being used by humans from a long time. Now they being widely used for power generation all around the world. Broadly wind mills are divided into Horizontal axis wind mills (HAWT) and Vertical axis wind mills (VAWT).Due to its design and rotation efficiency, the vertical axis wind turbine is known to be less efficient. Various researchers have done study on them for improving design and performance but there is very small data available on the various the effect of wind pressure and velocity. The numerical analysis is performed using Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) in ANSYS FLUENT 19.2 with the SST K-omega and K-epsilon turbulence model. Drag force, lift force, maximum and minimum pressure, and velocity are all calculated numerically.
